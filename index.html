our menu

pizza

Pizza is an Italian food that was created in Italy (The Naples area). It is made with different toppings. Some of the most common toppings are cheese,
sausages, pepperoni, vegetables, tomatoes, spices and herbs and basil.
These toppings are added over a piece of bread covered with sauce.

pasta

Pasta is a food made from flour, starch and water.
It is usually cooked in hot water before being eaten.
Pasta has been very popular in Italy and has also been eaten in many parts
of Asia for a long time.

burger

Burgers are one of the most convenient and easiest foods to eat
on the fly when we're in a hurry. We can have a burger at any time of day,
whether it's breakfast, lunch, or supper,
and maybe some fries and a Coke to go with it.
